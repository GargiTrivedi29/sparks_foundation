FLOOD RELIEF FUND

Donation For Survival

Making a donation is the ultimate sign of solidarity. Actions speak louder than words.The donation made by people will help directly to the flood prone area.Even your small donation can save thousands of lives.“No one has ever become poor by giving.” We supported the affected families with ready to eat food that could support a family of 5 for a week. The kit also included hygiene materials to ensure their safety during this flood.

How were your donations used?

  • Each kit (for a family of 5) contains a combination of ready to eat food and ration. Each kit for a family costs ₹500. One relief kit helps a family of five with 3 meals a day for a week.
  • The kits include ration kits, ready to eat packets of food, dry snacks such as Chiddwa (flattened rice), Moori (puffed rice), biscuits, Jaggery, Milk powder, bread and drinking water.
  • 100% of the funds were used in procuring and distributing these kits to affected families. We will disclose the use of proceeds in a financial transparency report sent to all contributors to the campaign.
  • Donations made towards this initiative are non-refundable. Receipts will be shared over mail within 10 days from the date of donation for all donations.
